Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

VoiceInk is built to be simple, fast, and private. No bloat, no accounts, no setup headaches.

touch_app

Instant Setup

Download, install, and start dictating in under a minute. No accounts, no sign-ups, no configuration needed.

keyboard

Push-to-Talk

Hold your shortcut key to speak, release to type. Your words appear instantly in any text field or app.

psychology

AI-Powered Accuracy

Powered by OpenAI's Whisper — choose from 9 models for the perfect balance of speed and accuracy.

translate

52+ Languages

Full multilingual support including English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Chinese, Hindi, Arabic, and many more.

dock

Lives in Your Menu Bar

A tiny floating capsule stays out of your way. It shows recording status with elegant animations — no clunky windows.

auto_fix_high

Smart Text Cleanup

Automatic removal of filler words ("um", "uh"), smart punctuation, and capitalization so your text reads naturally.

mic

Mic Selection

Choose from any connected microphone with a real-time audio level meter. Built-in or external — your choice.

tune

Customizable Shortcuts

Pick from preset hotkeys (⌥Space, ⌃Space, ⌥⇧Space) or configure your own to fit your workflow perfectly.

Privacy First

Your voice stays
on your device.

We believe voice data is deeply personal. That's why VoiceInk processes everything locally on your machine. No servers, no analytics, no telemetry — ever.

shield
100% Offline Processing

All speech recognition runs on your CPU using Whisper AI. Your audio never touches the internet.

analytics
Zero Analytics & Tracking

No usage analytics, no crash reporters, no telemetry of any kind. We literally can't see what you do.

dns
No Servers, No Accounts

There is no backend. No servers to hack. No accounts to breach. Your data exists only on your machine.

visibility_off
No Data Collection — Period

We don't collect your voice, your text, your usage patterns, or anything else. What happens on your machine stays there.

cloud_off No Cloud
wifi_off Works Offline
lock Your Data, Your Device
gpp_good Zero Telemetry
